## DAX Measures

- **Total Requests**  
  Counts all service requests.

- **Closed Requests**  
  Requests with Status = "Closed".

- **Open Requests**  
  Requests with Status = "Open" or "In Progress".

- **Avg Resolution Days**  
  Average ResolutionDays for closed requests.

- **Median Resolution Days**  
  Median ResolutionDays for closed requests.

- **SLA % Met**  
  Percentage of closed requests resolved within SLA target.
